In addition to the main conference topics, the conference committee invites submissions of specialist mini tracks. To submit a mini-track please e-mail brics-ic@hse.ru with your suggestion. We would like to receive one paragraph describing suggested mini track topics by April, 15. The idea of a mini track is to focus on a BRICS Global Business & Innovation topic that you are actively involved in and which you think you can attract a particular group of people/researchers to the conference. If your topic will be selected you will then need to provide us with a call for papers to go on to the web.
Academic research, case studies and work-in- progress are welcomed approaches. PhD Research, proposals for roundtable discussions, non-academic contributions and product demonstrations based on the main themes are also invited.
